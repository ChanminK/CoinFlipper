Overview

This Slack bot responds to @mentions, supports handy slash commands, and offers basic interactive UI (buttons & a modal). It works in channels (when invited) and in direct messages (DMs).

Mention Say @bot hi to get started.
Slash Try /hello or /flip.
UI Buttons + a simple form (modal).
Scope General chat; respectful use only.

Using the Bot

  • Invite to a channel: /invite @Gambler
  • Mention: type @Gambler then your message. The bot replies in the thread and may show buttons.
  • Direct Messages: open the bot in Apps → send messages privately.

Commands

Slash Commands

CommandWhat it doesWhereExample
/hello Sends a quick greeting. Channel or DM /hello
/flip Flips a virtual coin and posts the result. Channel or DM /flip → Heads or Tails
Slash commands always acknowledge instantly. If nothing happens, see Troubleshooting.

@Mention Triggers

  • General mention: @Gambler → the bot replies and shows buttons.
  • Ping button: returns a private “pong” to you (others won’t see it).
  • Open modal: launches a small form (collects your name) then sends you a DM.

Tips

  • Use threads: Please we don't want a repeat of the secret button.
  • DMs vs channels: DM to tell it your secrets. Channel to play against your friends.
  • Be specific: when doing a command, make sur every letter is lowercase.

Troubleshooting

  • “I can’t find the bot” → LITERALLY SEARCH UP GAMBLER IN APPS
  • “The bot isn’t responding” → It's probably asleep. Try /invite @Gambler and get better.
  • “Certain commands don't work” → uhhh probably I disabled something. Try messaging @Iamalive for more info
