Reactions

The bot adds reactions to messages from opted-in players (rate-limited):

  • “coin” → adds a coin reaction.
  • “gamble”, “gambling” → hype reply (e.g., “let’s go gambling!”) + reaction.
  • “win”, “won” → adds Heidi happy (custom) or a celebratory fallback.
  • “lose”, “lost” → adds Orpheus sad (custom) or a sad fallback.

Note: Custom emoji must exist in your workspace; otherwise fallbacks are used.

Games

Flip a Coin

  • Call first: choose Heads or Tails, then flip.
  • Payout: even money (1:1). Optional house rules may apply.
  • Play modes: solo vs dealer, or PvP via /challenge.
  • Rematch: “Double or Nothing” button appears for 60s after results.

Old Maid (Joker Version)

  • Setup: each player gets 4 cards (Joker in the deck).
  • Turn: on your turn, pick 1 random card from the other player’s hand; both players discard any new pairs.
  • Per-turn removal: 1 card is removed from each hand per turn (never the Joker).
  • Goal: don’t be the last player holding the Joker.

Poker (Regular)

  • Mode: classic poker (start with heads-up Texas Hold’em as default).
  • Cards: DM for hole cards; shared cards posted in thread; actions via buttons.
  • Emojis: optional emoji-based visual cues during hands.

Typing Battle

  • Prompt: both players get the same sentence from a rotating bank.
  • Goal: fastest correct typer wins. Minor typos may void the attempt.
  • Modes: PvP via /challenge or vs dealer (time target).

Battle & Challenges

  1. Start: /challenge @username or /challenge dealer, pick a game and stake.
  2. Accept: the other side gets Accept/Decline. If no response by timeout, it cancels.
  3. Lock stake: on accept, both sides’ stakes are reserved until outcome.
  4. Play: the game runs in a thread; turns use buttons, timers, and DMs where needed.
  5. Result: winner gets the whole pot; loser loses their stake.
  6. Rematch: “Double or Nothing” appears for 60s. Both must confirm.

You can challenge the dealer (AI-hosted) or another player. The bot stays within Slack’s capabilities for interactivity and DMs.

Shop & Power-Ups

  • Streak Saver: prevents a streak break once (consumes on use).
  • Game Breaker: void one finished game within 5 minutes; stake refunded to both; logged.
  • Sigma: your coins are not reset on the next weekly reset.
  • Reactor: the bot will respond to every message you send with over-the-top praise/glaze (rate-limited to avoid spam).

Cosmetics in Slack are limited. The bot can add special reactions, badges in App Home, and themed message headers—changing user profiles is generally not allowed by bots.

Streaks

  • Daily activity (ET): your streak increases if you play a game, issue a qualifying command, or claim the daily grant that day.
  • Reset: if no qualifying action in a calendar day (ET), your streak resets—unless a Streak Saver auto-protects it.
  • Stats: track current streak and longest streak; view with /streak.

Leaderboard & Announcements

  • /leaderboard: public top 10; shows your personal rank as “You are #N”.
  • Daily (12:00 AM ET): bot posts top 10 with streaks in the designated channel.
  • Weekly (Mon 12:00 AM ET): announces winners and the lowest coin holder; coins reset (Sigma holders carry over).

Coins & Economy

  • Daily grant: +100 coins to everyone opted into PLAY.
  • Balance check: /coin shows your current balance and streak summary.
  • Betting rule: you may start a bet only if your balance is currently ≥ 0. You can go negative as a result of losing.
  • Limits: optional per-bet/day caps may apply; all transactions are logged for fairness.

Secret Coins

  • There are exactly 3 Secret Coins.
  • Odds: ~1 in 1,000,000 per qualifying action (e.g., a command, game result).
  • On discovery: the bot DMs you privately so you can brag if you want.
  • No weekly reset: Secret Coins persist through resets.
  • Collect all 3: the bot publicly announces it, and you receive 1,000,000 coins.
